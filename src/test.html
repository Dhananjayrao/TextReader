How To Use

Getting Started

To get started, Insert a pdf file you wish to read and click View PDF The translucent gray pointer indicates where your gaze or hand movement is registered

Gesture : Navigation using your hands

Next Page

  • To navigate to the next page, make a pinching gesture using your right index finger and your thumb anywhere in the portion to the right of your page
  • Hold this pinching gesture, drag your hand until you reach inside the page
  • Release your pinched hand and you should be navigated to the next page

Previous Page

  • To navigate to the next page, make a pinching gesture using your left index finger and your thumb anywhere in the portion to the left of your page
  • Make a pinching gesture using your right index finger and your thumb, drag your hand until you reach inside the page
  • Release your pinched hand and you should be navigated to the previous page

Close File

  • To close the file and move back to the home screen, make a closed hand gesture by touching all of your finger tips together and maintain this gesture for a couple of seconds until you are navigated back to the home screen

Gaze : Navigation using your eyes

Next Page

  • Stare at the right side of the screen for a second, anywhere after the pdf page ends to move to the next page

Previous Page

  • Stare at the left side of the screen for a second, anywhere before the pdf starts to move to the previous page

Close File

  • To close the file and move back to the home screen, close your eyes for a couple of seconds until you are navigated back to the home screen
